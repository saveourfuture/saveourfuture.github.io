Find out how to reduce your footprint


Go easy on the showers - Reducing the amount of times you bath or shower a day will save water

Unplug those devices - Tell mom and dad to unplug any appliances that are not used during the day. TV's DVD players, Cable TV and even your water heater.

Get your family to use the car less - why not take a walk to the shops next time.

Remember to recycle whatever materials you can, like bottles, cans, and paper, so they can be collected and remade into new products.

Schools can save energy, preserve natural resources, and prevent greenhouse gas emissions by reducing, reusing, and recycling.

Can your school or home generate its own renewable energy? Talk with your family and school about the possibility of installing solar panels, a solar water heater, or even a wind turbine.
